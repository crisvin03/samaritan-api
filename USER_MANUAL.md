## Introduction

The Samaritan Bayanihan Community Management System is a web-based platform designed to help barangay (village) communities manage benefits, contributions, announcements, and member information efficiently.

### System Overview

The system supports three types of users:

- **Members:** Community members who can apply for benefits, submit contributions, and view announcements
- **Treasurers:** Barangay-level administrators who manage members, validate contributions, and forward benefit requests
- **Admins:** System administrators with full access to manage all aspects of the system

## Getting Started

### Accessing the System

1. Open your web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, or Safari)
2. Navigate to the system URL (provided by your administrator)
3. You'll see the landing page with login options

### Registration (For Members)

1. Click the **"Register"** button on the landing page
2. Fill in the registration form:
   - Full Name
   - Email Address
   - Password (minimum 8 characters)
   - Confirm Password
   - Phone Number
   - Barangay (select from dropdown)
   - Address
   - Birth Date
   - Gender
3. Click **"Register"**
4. Check your email for verification code
5. Enter the verification code to activate your account
6. Log in with your credentials

### Logging In

1. Click **"Login"** on the landing page
2. Select your user type:
   - **Member Login**
   - **Treasurer Login**
   - **Admin Login**
3. Enter your email and password
4. Click **"Login"**

### Password Reset

If you forgot your password:

1. Click **"Forgot Password?"** on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for the reset link
4. Click the link and enter your new password

## Member Guide

### Dashboard

After logging in, you'll see your member dashboard showing:
- **Quick Stats:** Your total contributions, pending benefits, and notifications
- **Recent Announcements:** Latest community announcements
- **Quick Actions:** Links to common tasks

### Profile Management

#### View Profile

1. Click **"Profile"** in the navigation menu
2. View your personal information:
   - Personal details
   - Contact information
   - Barangay information
   - Account status

#### Edit Profile

1. Go to **Profile** > **Edit Profile**
2. Update any of the following:
   - Phone number
   - Address
   - Profile picture
3. Click **"Save Changes"**

#### Change Password

1. Go to **Profile** > **Change Password**
2. Enter your current password
3. Enter your new password (minimum 8 characters)
4. Confirm your new password
5. Click **"Update Password"**

#### Upload Documents

1. Go to **Profile** > **Upload Documents**
2. Select document type:
   - ID Document (Government-issued ID)
   - Proof of Address
   - Other supporting documents
3. Click **"Choose File"** and select your document
4. Click **"Upload"**

**Accepted formats:** PDF, JPG, PNG (Max 5MB per file)

### Benefit Applications

#### Apply for a Benefit

1. Navigate to **Benefits** > **Apply for Benefit**
2. Select the benefit type:
   - **Hospitalization:** Medical expenses for hospitalization
   - **Burial:** Funeral and burial assistance
   - **Animal Bite:** Medical expenses for animal bite treatment
   - **Accidental:** Assistance for accidents
   - **Outpatient:** Outpatient medical expenses
   - **Birthday:** Birthday gift benefit
3. Fill in the required information:
   - **Requested Amount:** Enter the amount you're requesting
   - **Reason:** Provide a detailed reason (minimum 10 characters)
   - **Supporting Documents:** Upload relevant documents (optional)
4. Click **"Submit Application"**

#### View My Benefit Requests

1. Go to **Benefits** > **My Requests**
2. View all your benefit applications with:
   - Application date
   - Benefit type
   - Requested amount
   - Current status
   - Actions available

#### Benefit Statuses

- **Pending:** Application submitted, awaiting review
- **Under Review:** Being reviewed by treasurer/admin
- **Approved:** Application approved, awaiting disbursement
- **Rejected:** Application rejected (check admin notes for reason)
- **Disbursed:** Benefit has been disbursed

#### View Benefit Details

1. Click on any benefit from **My Requests**
2. View complete details:
   - Application information
   - Status history
   - Admin notes (if any)
   - Supporting documents
   - Approved amount (if approved)

#### Update Benefit Request

You can only update **pending** benefit requests:

1. Go to **Benefits** > **My Requests**
2. Find a pending request
3. Click **"Edit"**
4. Update the information
5. Click **"Update"**

### Contributions

#### Submit a Contribution

1. Navigate to **Contributions** > **Submit Contribution**
2. Select contribution type:
   - **Weekly Savings:** Regular weekly contribution
   - **Special Contribution:** One-time special contribution
3. Enter the amount
4. Upload proof of payment (optional but recommended)
5. Add notes (optional)
6. Click **"Submit Contribution"**

#### View My Contributions

1. Go to **Contributions** > **My Contributions**
2. View all your contributions:
   - Date submitted
   - Amount
   - Type
   - Status (Pending/Validated/Rejected)
   - Validation date

#### Contribution Statuses

- **Pending:** Awaiting validation by treasurer
- **Validated:** Contribution has been validated
- **Rejected:** Contribution was rejected (check notes)

#### Export Contribution History

1. Go to **Contributions** > **My Contributions**
2. Click **"Export"** button
3. Download your contribution history as PDF

### Passbook

View your complete contribution history:

1. Navigate to **Passbook**
2. See a chronological record of:
   - All contributions
   - Running balance
   - Transaction dates
   - Status of each contribution

### Announcements

#### View Announcements

1. Go to **Announcements**
2. View all community announcements:
   - Announcement title
   - Content
   - Date posted
   - Priority level (High/Medium/Low)

#### View Announcement Details

1. Click on any announcement
2. Read the full content
3. View posting date and priority

### Notifications

#### View Notifications

1. Click the **bell icon** in the top navigation
2. View all your notifications:
   - New announcements
   - Benefit status updates
   - Contribution validation updates
   - System messages

#### Mark as Read

- Click on a notification to mark it as read
- Click **"Mark All as Read"** to mark all notifications as read

## Treasurer Guide

### Dashboard

The treasurer dashboard shows:
- **Barangay Statistics:** Total members, contributions, and benefits
- **Pending Tasks:** Contributions and benefits requiring action
- **Recent Activity:** Latest member activities

### Member Management

#### View Members

1. Navigate to **Members**
2. View all members in your barangay:
   - Member name
   - Email
   - Status (Active/Suspended)
   - Total contributions
   - Total benefits

#### View Member Details

1. Click on any member
2. View complete information:
   - Personal details
   - Contribution history
   - Benefit applications
   - Account status

#### Activate/Suspend Member

1. Go to member details
2. Click **"Activate"** or **"Suspend"** button
3. Confirm the action

### Contribution Management

#### View Contributions

1. Navigate to **Contributions**
2. View all contributions from your barangay members:
   - Member name
   - Amount
   - Type
   - Date submitted
   - Status

#### Validate Contribution

1. Go to **Contributions**
2. Find a pending contribution
3. Click **"Validate"**
4. Review the contribution details
5. Add validation notes (optional)
6. Click **"Validate Contribution"** or **"Reject"**

#### Reject Contribution

1. Find the contribution
2. Click **"Reject"**
3. Enter rejection reason
4. Click **"Reject Contribution"**

### Benefit Management

#### View Benefits

1. Navigate to **Benefits**
2. View all benefit applications from your barangay:
   - Member name
   - Benefit type
   - Requested amount
   - Status
   - Date applied

#### Review Benefit Application

1. Click on a benefit application
2. Review:
   - Application details
   - Supporting documents
   - Member information
3. Take action:
   - **Forward to Admin:** Forward for final approval
   - **Reject:** Reject the application (with reason)

#### Forward to Admin

1. Review the benefit application
2. Click **"Forward to Admin"**
3. Add notes (optional)
4. Click **"Forward"**

The application will be sent to admin for final approval.

#### Reject Benefit

1. Review the application
2. Click **"Reject"**
3. Enter rejection reason
4. Click **"Reject Application"**

### Announcements

#### Create Announcement

1. Navigate to **Announcements** > **Create Announcement**
2. Fill in the form:
   - **Title:** Announcement title
   - **Content:** Full announcement content
   - **Priority:** High/Medium/Low
   - **Target Audience:** All members or specific groups
3. Click **"Publish"** or **"Save as Draft"**

#### Manage Announcements

1. Go to **Announcements**
2. View all your announcements
3. Actions available:
   - **Edit:** Modify announcement
   - **Publish/Unpublish:** Change publication status
   - **Delete:** Remove announcement

### Reports

#### View Reports

1. Navigate to **Reports**
2. Access different report types:
   - **Financial Report:** Contribution and benefit summaries
   - **Member Report:** Member statistics
   - **Benefit Report:** Benefit distribution analysis

#### Export Reports

1. Generate the desired report
2. Click **"Export"** button
3. Download as PDF or Excel

### Notifications

Treasurers receive notifications for:
- New contribution submissions
- New benefit applications
- Member status changes
- System updates

## Troubleshooting

### Login Issues

**Problem:** Cannot log in  
**Solutions:**
- Verify email and password are correct
- Check if account is activated
- Try password reset
- Contact administrator if account is suspended

**Problem:** "Email not verified" message  
**Solution:**
- Check email for verification code
- Request new verification code
- Contact admin if email not received

### Upload Issues

**Problem:** File upload fails  
**Solutions:**
- Check file size (max 5MB)
- Verify file format (PDF, JPG, PNG)
- Check internet connection
- Try a different browser

### Notification Issues

**Problem:** Not receiving notifications  
**Solutions:**
- Check browser notifications are enabled
- Refresh the page
- Check notification settings
- Clear browser cache

### Display Issues

**Problem:** Page not displaying correctly  
**Solutions:**
- Clear browser cache
- Try a different browser
- Check internet connection
- Contact administrator

## FAQs

### General Questions

**Q: How do I change my password?**  
A: Go to Profile > Change Password and follow the instructions.

**Q: Can I edit my benefit application after submitting?**  
A: Only pending applications can be edited. Once under review, contact your treasurer or admin.

**Q: How long does benefit approval take?**  
A: Typically 3-5 business days, depending on the complexity and required documentation.

**Q: What documents do I need for benefit application?**  
A: Required documents vary by benefit type. Check with your treasurer or admin for specific requirements.

**Q: How do I know if my contribution was validated?**  
A: You'll receive a notification, and the status will change to "Validated" in your contributions list.

**Q: Can I view my contribution history?**  
A: Yes, go to Passbook to view your complete contribution history.

**Q: What should I do if I forgot my password?**  
A: Click "Forgot Password?" on the login page and follow the email instructions.

**Q: How do I update my profile information?**  
A: Go to Profile > Edit Profile and update the information you want to change.

**Q: Why can't I see some features?**  
A: Features are role-based. Contact your administrator if you believe you should have access to certain features.

**Q: How do I contact support?**  
A: Contact your barangay treasurer or system administrator for assistance.

### Technical Questions

**Q: What browsers are supported?**  
A: Chrome, Firefox, Edge, and Safari (latest versions).

**Q: Do I need to install any software?**  
A: No, the system runs entirely in your web browser.

**Q: Is my data secure?**  
A: Yes, the system uses secure encryption and follows data protection best practices.

**Q: Can I access the system on my phone?**  
A: Yes, the system is fully responsive and works on mobile devices.

## Glossary

- **Barangay:** Smallest administrative division in the Philippines (village/ward)
- **Benefit:** Financial assistance provided to members
- **Contribution:** Regular savings or special payments made by members
- **Disbursement:** The act of releasing approved benefit funds
- **Validation:** Confirmation that a contribution has been received and verified
- **Treasurer:** Barangay-level administrator responsible for financial management
- **Admin:** System administrator with full access
